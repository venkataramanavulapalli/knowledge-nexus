🚀 Space Technology

Space technology includes the tools humans build to explore beyond Earth, such as rockets, satellites, probes, telescopes, and space stations.

Rockets are used to escape Earth’s gravity and carry spacecraft into orbit or beyond. Satellites help with communication, weather tracking, navigation, and scientific research.

Space telescopes like Hubble and James Webb let scientists observe distant galaxies, nebulae, and exoplanets with incredible detail.

As technology improves, space exploration becomes more powerful, helping humans understand the universe and possibly travel farther than ever before.
